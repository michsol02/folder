SMEs

Responsible 100 helps your small or medium-sized enterprise to be more responsible, and for your increased responsibility to drive commercial success and financial results.

Understanding and addressing a constantly evolving set of pressing social, environmental, ethical and governance issues is a complex and often overwhelming undertaking for any business. We help you to better understand the responsibility challenges you face, how well you perform, and what you can do to improve. Discover our service information.

Services for SMEs

We define small and medium sized businesses as those with 250 employees or less.

1

WHICH ISSUES MATTER MOST TO MY COMPANY?

Determine the relevance and importance of a range of social, environmental and ethical issues to you and your business. We help businesses identify which issues matter most through a short, confidential and no-obligation survey, and associated analysis and personalised report.

2

HOW IS MY BUSINESS DOING?

Responsible 100 provide self assessment tools and guidance to reveal how your business measures up on the issues that matter most, how you compare to your peers, and where improvements can be made.

3

HOW CAN WE IMPROVE?

You now know your priorities and with those in mind together we set your goals and necessary steps to make the greatest positive impact. A tailored improvement plan with clear, achievable milestones. Responsible 100 are there to guide and provide a regular check up to ensure you and your colleagues reach your targets.

4

BEST PRACTICE GUIDELINES

Responsible 100 scorecards provide a comprehensive account of what POOR, OKAY, GOOD and EXCELLENT performance looks like. We explore over 50 issues across eight broad responsibility categories: Community, Environment, Ethical Choices, Finance and Governance, Human Rights, Marketplace Ethics, Technology, and Workforce. The R100 scorecard library constitutes an incredibly valuable resource. See the full issues list.

5

CONSULTANCY

We offer consultancy and support for adoption and integration of improved policies and practices. Having helped to diagnose problems and areas of underperformance, and the appropriate solutions, our experts help clients to plan and deliver the required changes.

6

REPORTING & COMMUNICATING

We help R100 organisations to effectively communicate the things they are doing well, and the areas in which they are improving. Options include: PR strategy, press release writing internal comms; traditional media targeting; social media targeting.

HOW IT WORKS

Responsible 100 is a pro-business, pro-profit and collaborative initiative, open to all. It includes businesses large and small, and from various sectors, as well as a wide range of leading NGOs and think tanks.

Through a managed process within a safe and supportive space, we're charting current business performance on a variety of critical social, environmental and ethical issues.
