OUR COMMUNITY

Responsible 100 is a community of organisations and individuals working together to help create businesses that can prosper, innovate and grow - in the true service of people and planet. 

  • WE BELIEVE - business has almost unbridled potential to make the world a better place
  • WE BELIEVE - now is the time to realise it, to avert crises which on their current trajectories will almost certainly result in catastrophic outcomes for humanity and the natural world
  • WE BELIEVE - the existential crises we face are not anyone’s fault, but they are everyone’s problem
  • WE BELIEVE - people’s opinions, choices and actions are critical to creating the necessary incentives and rewards for businesses to increase their positive impacts on the environment and society, and decrease their negative impacts
  • WE BELIEVE - norms do shift, and in respect of acceptable business practices, must shift again now
  • WE BELIEVE - profits that can only be accrued by offloading costs onto people or planet or by exploiting vulnerable groups - even if it has been legal or customary to do this for years - can no longer be legitimately earned
  • WE BELIEVE - like Jonathan Pie, that “shampoos or low fat alternatives to butter” whose production requires the destruction of the Amazon rainforest or that “four species of frog are extinguished out of existence” shouldn’t be made
  • WE BELIEVE - businesses that exploit the pandemic and bribe or influence elected representatives to dishonestly obtain lucrative public contracts, as recently highlighted by Dale Vince, offend public decency
  • WE BELIEVE - despite the obvious moral case, it is only the economic case that has the power to deliver change at the speed and breadth now required 
  • WE BELIEVE - responsibility is a skill, one that any business can learn and improve on, to increase its knowledge, competence and confidence, to be ever bolder in its actions and decisions, and to deploy its resources ever more efficiently and effectively
  • WE BELIEVE - through collaboration with its stakeholders, any business can determine and achieve an optimal balance between delivering its goods and services and pursuing commercial success on one hand, with contribution to people and planet on the other
  • WE BELIEVE - soon, real responsibility will drive up profits, and irresponsible practices will diminish them
JOIN US

Responsible 100 is the result of collaboration with individuals from the widest range of businesses and civil society organisations.

Our aim for the near future is to enable anyone to participate in the work we do. The more diverse our community the better. We welcome consumers, employees, investors, as well as activists, campaigners and citizens.

Irrespective of the hat or hats you wear, if you’d like to help unlock the vast potential of business to fix the way the world works, you’ll be more than welcome.

Working together, identifying opportunities and co-creating and trialing solutions is Responsible 100’s unique approach as well as the ethos of our community.
