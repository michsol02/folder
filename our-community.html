GET STARTED

We’ve created a “people’s assembly” of individuals from the widest range of businesses and civil society organisations. Our aim for the near future is to enable anyone to participate in the work we do.

The more diverse this community the better. It will continue to include leaders from businesses and NGOs. But also employees, investors and consumers. As well as community members, citizens, mothers and fathers, children and grandparents.

Irrespective of the hat or hats you wear, if you’d like to help unlock the vast potential of business to fix the way the world works, you’ll be more than welcome.

Covid 19 has forced us to create new ways of doing the work we used to do face to face. But at the same time, these changes will democratise, open up and thus improve Responsible 100.

As before, we’ll map out the possible pathways to achieve better social and environmental practices and outcomes, on dozens of important responsibility and sustainability issues. But we’ll use technology in new ways, and across almost all activities.

Working together, identifying opportunities and co-creating and trialling solutions is Responsible 100’s unique approach as well as the ethos of our community.

BRANDS, SMALL & MEDIUM FIRMS

SUPPORTER ORGANISATIONS

More than ever, it is essential that the global challenges the world faces are championed by passionate NGOs and campaigners. Responsible 100 enables campaign groups to raise their issues right up to the top of boardroom agendas in businesses right around the world.

We have a proud record of enabling those holding corporate power to account to partner and collaborate with the very businesses they scrutinise. Supporter organisations are integral to our approach and model, and to identifying and influencing positive change in business.

"Corporate reponsibility efforts are often regarded as greenwash because they fail to explore and reveal what really happens on the ground - Responsible 100 identifies and reflects reality "
