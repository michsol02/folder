HOW WE WORK

Each participant company shares its responsibility data through a managed, supported process. Responsible 100 uses this to create benchmarks to help all businesses to improve.

1. DETERMINE YOUR PRIORITY ISSUES 

20 responsibility issues across 8 different categories are presented. 

Respondents are asked to indicate whether they consider the issue to be either:

>>  CRITICAL
>>  IMPORTANT
>>  A FACTOR
>>  NO IMPACT 

2. SCORE YOUR CURRENT PERFORMANCE

We help businesses to reflect on the things they do and provide brief details. Along with a short written description of their current policies and practices, the company is supported in objectively categorising how it currently performs as either:

>>  EXCELLENT
>>  GOOD
>>  OKAY
>>  POOR 

These initial, approximate scores can subsequently be changed up or down when additional details are submitted and when there are further comparisons to consider.

3. YOUR FIRST "RESPONSIBILITY INDICATOR"

Your first Responsibility Indicator is created within an hour, and then updated and improved on an ongoing basis. It immediately reveals the issues on which further effort and focus are needed most, e.g. where priority level is high but performance is poor. It enables businesses to map out their current performance and future plans. It underpins ongoing improvement. 

4. DEVELOPING LIVE CASE STUDIES

Responsible 100 supports businesses in developing their basic written disclosures into complete, live case studies.
 
These may shared and developed internally. Then they may be shared and developed externally as other businesses, partners and stakeholders are invited in, according to your preferences.

5. NETWORKED PERFORMANCE MAPS

Businesses participating in Responsible 100 map and master their own performance through their Responsibility Indicators and the accompanying improvement plans we help them to create. They also get to see - and learn from - many examples of POOR, OKAY, GOOD and EXCELLENT practices that other participants provide.

6. EXPERT HELP TO IMPROVE

Responsible 100 partners with incredibly impressive and inspiring issue experts.

The brilliant, passionate people we work with provide you with bespoke help and guidance.

Together, we'll help you to improve your performance on all your priority issues.

7. CREATING CONSENSUS

You'll decide how much of your company information to release, when, and to which members of our community. Responsible 100 makes the process of opening up safe and beneficial. It promotes collaboration and builds consensus.

Admitting your performance is below where you wish it to be is hard to do. But it is not impossible. The stark reality is that all businesses are POOR on numerous issues. Saying so, and revealing how you will go about improving, is true accountability on which enduring trust is built.
