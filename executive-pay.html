> Executive Pay
Introducing The Issue 

Executive pay is financial compensation which when combined with other non-financial benefits constitutes total executive compensation and is received by an executive from their employing organisation in return for their labour. It is typically a mixture of fixed salary and variable performance-based bonuses and benefits and other perquisites. Each element is ideally configured to take into account law, government regulations, taxation rules, the desires of the organisation and of its executive.
