> Trading With Oppressive Regimes
Introducing the issue 

When it comes to the disregard for and abuse of human rights and norms, nation states such as China and Saudi Arabia have frequently sprung to mind in recent years. But now it is Russia that stands out as the preeminent oppressive, aggressive and autocratic state on the global map. With global governments and international organisations struggling to hold offenders accountable, many have advocated for businesses to use their voices to make change.

Questions to answer 

Q.1 Is your business supporting the Russian economy, directly or indirectly?

Q.2 Is your business complicit in the maintenance of this or any other oppressive regime?
