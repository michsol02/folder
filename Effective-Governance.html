Introducing The Issue 
Organisational governance concerns the creation of checks, balances and controls on which proper, honest and effective management depends. Massive financial losses and business frauds, scandals and cover ups are often the result of governance failures, such as a weak or dysfunctional boards of directors failing to do their jobs properly.
