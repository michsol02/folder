SIMPLE STEPS

Businesses participating in Responsible 100 derive various benefits from the steps they undertake and the information they share. And in so doing, they map out the wide range of possible responses to the most important social, environmental and ethical issues that exist in the world. These crowd-sourced, ever-developing, networked benchmarks are immensely valuable resources for all those keen to discover and promote the highest standards of business responsibility.

1. DETERMINE YOUR PRIORITY ISSUES 

20 responsibility issues across 8 different categories are presented. 

Respondents are asked to indicate whether they consider the issue to be either:

+  CRITICAL
+  IMPORTANT
+  A FACTOR or
+  NO IMPACT 

2. SCORE YOUR CURRENT PERFORMANCE

We help businesses to reflect on the things they do and provide brief details.

Along with a short written description of their current policies and practices, the company is supported in objectively categorising how it currently performs as either:

+  EXCELLENT
+  GOOD
+  OKAY
+  POOR 

This approximate score can subsequently be changed up or down when there are more details and comparisons to consider. 

3. YOUR FIRST "RESPONSIBILITY INDICATOR"

Your first Responsibility Indicator is created within an hour, and then updated and improved regularly. It immediately reveals the issues on which further effort and focus are needed most (i.e. where priority level is high but performance is poor). It enables businesses to map out their current performance and future plans. And it underpins ongoing improvement. 

4. DEVELOPING LIVE CASE STUDIES

Responsible 100 supports businesses in developing their basic written disclosures into complete, live case studies.
 
These may shared and developed internally. Then they may be shared and developed externally as other businesses, partners and stakeholders are invited in, according to your preferences.

5. NETWORKED PERFORMANCE MAPS

Businesses participating in Responsible 100 not only map and master their own performance through their Responsibility Indicators and the improvement plans we help them to create on the back of them, they also get to see - and learn from - many examples of POOR, OKAY, GOOD and EXCELLENT practices that other participants provide.

6. EXPERT HELP TO IMPROVE

Responsible 100 partners with genuinely impressive and inspiring issue experts.

These brilliant, passionate people provide you with bespoke help and guidance to improve from POOR to OKAY to GOOD to EXCELLENT, on all your priority issues, as and where it is appropriate and possible for your business to do so.

7. CREATING CONSENSUS

Accounting for POOR performance is hard to do, but not impossible. The stark reality is that all businesses are POOR on numerous issues. Admitting as much, and revealing your intended path to OKAY and beyond, is true accountability which builds trust

You'll decide how much of your company information to release, when, and to which members of our community. Responsible 100 makes the process of opening up safe and beneficial. It promotes collaboration and builds consensus.
