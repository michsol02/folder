HOW IT WORKS
1. R100'S SIMPLE STEPS 

1. ASSESS your immediate priorities

2. INVITE your stakeholders to support and participate 

3. BENCHMARK your performance - versus peers and over time

4. IMPROVE your impact on your priority issue areas

5. REVEAL details of the progress and impact you make

First, consider the social, environmental and ethical issues that affect your company. We currently explore 30 separate responsibility issues, across 8 categories. Working out how to prioritise them is our starting point. This can be done by one responsible person within a company, or better by several people, and ideally with everyone in the business invited to get involved and express their views as to which responsibility issues are important, and which are not, for the company to be abreast of.


2. USE THE R100 INSIDE LOGO

RESPONSIBLE 100 INSIDE
SOCIALLY & ENVIRONMENTALLY AWARE

As soon as your are comfortable and confident that Responsible 100 is right for your business you'll be able to display the Responsible 100 inside logo that states simply that your organisation is socially and environmentally aware.

GETTING STARTED couldn't be easier. We ask you to simply consider some of the social, environmental and ethical issues that affect your company, and indicate which are the most important. We currently explore 30 separate responsibility issues, across 8 categories. 


3. RESPONSIBILITY INDICATOR

The Responsibility Indicator is at the heart of what we do. It is a powerful dashboard and management tool. It is a constantly evolving record and repository of your priorities, practices, risks and opportunities. 

Your first Responsibility Indicator is created in minutes, and updated and improved on an ongoing basis.

Working out how to prioritise them is our starting point. This can be done by one responsible person within a company, or better by several people, and ideally with everyone in the business invited to get involved and express their views as to which responsibility issues are important, and which are not, for the company to be abreast of.


4. R100 INSIDE & OUT LOGO

RESPONSIBLE 100 INSIDE & OUT SOCIALLY & ENVIRONMENTALLY ACCOUNTABLE

Once you've begun to publicly share some of your outputs you'll be able to display the Responsible 100 inside and out logo that states that your organisation is socially and environmentally accountable.

Action and improvement are your ultimate goals. This multi-step process determines where they should be attempted first.

Businesses don't choose which issue areas to improve on, the issue areas really choose the business. Initially, this is very likely to be on issues of the highest priority, where current performance is POOR, where change is desirable, achievable, affordable, appropriate, and where consequential increased positive impacts on people and planet - and decreased negative impacts - are very probable. Responsible 100 will confirm all the above in the scoping and planning stages, and then support you in making changes to policy and practice to improve.

As performance is improved on such issues, you'll next invest in improvement on issues of slightly lower priority, or where your performance is already OKAY at least, or where change is more difficult, or expensive, or the desired outcomes or improvements are less certain to be achieved.

Responsible 100 supports this systematic and relentless approach to performing as well as possible, across everything, knowing full well that everything at once is impossible.

"Responsible 100 enables companies to very consciously and actively be part of something better. To balance their commercial objectives with the needs of people and planet. And to work hard at it, to get better at it, and to reap due reward, rather than just hoping or expecting everything will somehow sort itself out."
Spokesperson, Extinction Rebellion
