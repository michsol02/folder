HOW IT WORKS
SIMPLE STEPS, START TODAY

It could not be more straightforward to get started with Responsible 100 and start your journey to greater responsibility.

GET STARTED NOW
1. ISSUES PRIORITISATION 

20 separate responsibility issues across 8 different categories are presented. 

Anyone in and around the business may offer their view as to how important each issue is to how the organisation operates.  

Issue by issue, some basic information is provided. It is simply people's personal views we seek to capture at this stage. There are no 'right' or 'wrong' responses. 

Respondents are asked to indicate whether they consider the issues to be CRITICAL, IMPORTANT, or only A FACTOR, or whether it has NO IMPACT at all to the business.

2. INVITE YOUR STAKEHOLDERS

Businesses often face unenviable choices.

Sharing these hard decisions by inviting stakeholders in to have their say builds consensus and community. It also socialises the challenges and prompts useful discussions.

It is never too soon to invite your stakeholders to join the conversation. Responsible 100 assists businesses in reaching out. Employees are usually the first stakeholder group to invite to consider which issues are top priority.   

3. ESTIMATE CURRENT PERFORMANCE

Responsible 100 identifies and collects the myriad of policies and practices that businesses adopt in response to the social, environmental and ethical challenges they face.

All business responses to all responsibility challenges are then categorised as POOR, OKAY, GOOD or EXCELLENT by the businesses themselves.

This is done rapidly in the first instance, and the scores can be changed up or down in due course as we explore more carefully with the businesses we work with.

4. YOUR FIRST RESPONSIBILITY INDICATOR

Your first Responsibility Indicator is created in minutes, and updated and improved on an ongoing basis.

5. DEVELOPING LIVE CASE STUDIES

Having created a first Responsibility Indicator, the deeper dives can begin.

Responsible 100 guides businesses through a process of learning more about the issues they need to address more closely, applying these learnings and insights to their own organisations and current circumstances, and then describing what they do, how and why. 

Quickly we co-develop live case studies that the business can further develop with its stakeholders, and then share internally or externally as it sees fit.   

6. WORKSHOPS WITH THE EXPERTS

This highly practical, ordered and supported approach to responsibility enables your business to thoroughly explore where it might improve.

Responsible 100 will also help you explore how to make changes, calling on the knowledge we have of other businesses facing similar challenges and circumstances. To augment this, we'll also call on our growing network of issues experts and a growing schedule of brilliant workshops. 

METHODICAL, ONGOING IMPROVEMENT

Ultimately, we want to answer your most critical of question of all: where should our business invest its precious, limited resources to improve our responsibility performance and benefit our bottom line?

Action and improvement are your ultimate goals. This efficient, structured process determines where they should be attempted first.

Work will usually start in respect of highest priority issues where your current performance is POOR, and where change is not only desirable but also achievable, affordable, appropriate, and where consequential increased positive impacts on people and planet - and decreased negative impacts - are very probable.

Responsible 100 will confirm all the above in the scoping and planning stages, and then support you in making changes to policy and practice to improve.

As performance is improved on such issues, you'll next invest in improvement on issues of slightly lower priority, or where your performance is already OKAY at least, or where change is more difficult, or expensive, or the desired outcomes or improvements are less certain to be achieved.

Responsible 100 supports this systematic and relentless approach to performing as well as possible, across everything, knowing full well that everything at once is impossible.

"Responsible 100 enables companies to very consciously and actively be part of something better. To balance their commercial objectives with the needs of people and planet. And to work hard at it, to get better at it, and to reap due reward, rather than just hoping or expecting everything will somehow sort itself out."
Spokesperson, Extinction Rebellion
