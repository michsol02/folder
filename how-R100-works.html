HOW IT WORKS
SIMPLE STEPS, START TODAY

It could not be more straightforward to get started with Responsible 100 and start your journey to greater responsibility.

GET STARTED NOW
1. ISSUES PRIORITISATION 

20 separate responsibility issues across 8 different categories are presented. 

Anyone in and around the business may offer their view as to how important each issue is to how the organisation operates.

Issue by issue, basic information is provided, with plenty more resources available in the following steps. 

It is simply people's personal views we seek to capture at this stage. There are no 'right' or 'wrong' responses. Respondents are asked to indicate whether they consider the issues to be CRITICAL, IMPORTANT, or only A FACTOR, or whether it has NO IMPACT at all to the business.

2. INVITE YOUR STAKEHOLDERS

Businesses often face unenviable choices.

By inviting stakeholders in to have their say, consensus and community can be established. It is a great way to share and socialise the challenges a business faces and prompts useful discussions.

It is never too soon to invite your stakeholders to join the conversation. Responsible 100 assists businesses in reaching out. Employees are usually the first stakeholder group to invite to consider which issues are top priority.   

3. ESTIMATE CURRENT PERFORMANCE

Responsible 100 helps businesses to reflect on the things they do and objectively categorise their current practices as either POOR, OKAY, GOOD or EXCELLENT.

This is done rapidly in the first instance, and the scores can be changed up or down in due course as we explore more carefully with the businesses we work with.

By working with businesses of all sizes and sectors, Responsible 100 identifies, collects and collates the myriad of policies and practices that businesses adopt in response to the social, environmental and ethical challenges they face.

4. YOUR FIRST RESPONSIBILITY INDICATOR

Your first Responsibility Indicator is created in minutes, and updated and improved on an ongoing basis.

5. DEVELOPING LIVE CASE STUDIES

Having created a first Responsibility Indicator we can immediately see where further exploration is needed most.

Responsible 100 guides businesses through a process of learning more about the issues they need to address more closely, applying these learnings and insights to their own organisations and current circumstances, and then describing what they do, how and why.   

Quickly we co-develop live case studies that the business can further develop with its stakeholders, and then share internally or externally as it sees fit.

6. HELP FROM THE EXPERTS

Responsible 100 enables you to discover where to improve, and also how to make changes.

We call on the knowledge we garner from of other businesses facing similar challenges in comparable circumstances, as contained in our benchmarks. To augment this, we also call on our growing network of issues experts with we deliver brilliant workshops and other direct means of support.

7. PARTNERING WITH STAKEHOLDERS

Issue by issue, your business will compile its live case studies, and do so with internal stakeholders where appropriate.

When the time is right, you'll want to share your story more widely, and invite the outside in. 

You'll decide whether to make some or all of your content available, and to whom. This process of opening up enables and promotes collaboration. This is true accountability which builds trust and partnership, reduces risks and promotes innovation.
