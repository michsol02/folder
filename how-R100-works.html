HOW IT WORKS
SIMPLE STEPS

1. ASSESS your immediate priorities

2. INVITE your stakeholders to support and participate

3. BENCHMARK your performance - versus peers and over time

4. IMPROVE your impact on your priority issue areas

5. REVEAL details of the progress and impact you make

GET STARTED RIGHT AWAY

It could not be more straightforward to get started with Responsible 100 and start your journey to greater responsibility.

You simply need to consider some of the social, environmental and ethical issues that affect your company, and decide which - in your own opinion - are the most important. We currently explore 30 separate responsibility issues, across 8 categories.

It plainly states that your business or organisation is socially and environmentally aware.

PROGRESS ON YOUR JOURNEY 

Responsible 100 provides you insight and control over your responsibility performance.

Your start can be made by one point person within your company, or with several people involved. Ideally, before long, everyone in the business can be invited to participate in various appropriate ways. Once your priority issues are identified, we’ll next work out how you currently perform.

We do this by prompting some basic disclosures as to relevant practices or policies - possibly involving a selection of employees or other stakeholders - and asking a few follow up questions. We’ll quickly build up a picture and basic written summary of your current policies and practices. These written accounts may form the basis for internal or external reporting in due course.

RESPONSIBILITY INDICATOR
The Responsibility Indicator is at the heart of what we do. It is a powerful dashboard and management tool. It is a constantly evolving record and repository of your priorities, practices, risks and opportunities. 

Your first Responsibility Indicator is created in minutes, and updated and improved on an ongoing basis. It combines a categorisation of 30 key issues by theme, according to their priority level to your business, and lastly according to your current performance which is measured as either POOR, OKAY, GOOD or EXCELLENT.

RESPONSIBLE 100 INSIDE & OUT
SOCIALLY & ENVIRONMENTALLY ACCOUNTABLE

At a certain point in your journey, you will be sharing outputs from the Responsible 100 process with internal and external stakeholders. We'll help you find your 'critical friends' and build lasting, valuable relationships with them. Once sharing some of your outputs you'll be able to display the Responsible 100 Inside & Out logo that states that your organisation is socially and environmentally accountable.

CONSTANT IMPROVEMENT

Ultimately we want to address that most critical of questions: where should our business invest its precious, limited resources to affect the most urgently needed change?

Action and improvement are your ultimate goals. This multi-step process determines where they should be attempted first.

It is usually in respect of highest priority issues where your current performance is POOR, and where change is not only desirable but also achievable, affordable, appropriate, and where consequential increased positive impacts on people and planet - and decreased negative impacts - are very probable.

Responsible 100 will confirm all the above in the scoping and planning stages, and then support you in making changes to policy and practice to improve.

As performance is improved on such issues, you'll next invest in improvement on issues of slightly lower priority, or where your performance is already OKAY at least, or where change is more difficult, or expensive, or the desired outcomes or improvements are less certain to be achieved. Responsible 100 supports this systematic and relentless approach to performing as well as possible, across everything, knowing full well that everything at once is impossible.
